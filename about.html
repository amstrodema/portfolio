With approximately a decade of experience in software development spanning various programming languages and platforms, I have cultivated a proficient skill set as a Full Stack Engineer.

My journey commenced with the development of applications using Java, Vb6, and procedural paradigm tools like Pascal. Subsequently, I transitioned to Microsoft's .Net C#. While working with Java, I gained expertise in Android Native Development, leveraging the capabilities of the Android Studio IDE.

Throughout my career, I have maintained a consistent progression with C#, starting from .Net 3.5 and extending to .Net Core 7. Armed with a solid foundation of knowledge, I have successfully engineered diverse Desktop Applications using Winform. In recent years, I have shifted my focus towards the development of Web-Based Applications, primarily utilizing .Net MVC, Angular 8 (Typescript) and currently, .Net Core 7.

Throughout my professional journey, I have had the privilege of working within diverse organizations, assuming a range of responsibilities. My experience extends across a multitude of sectors, including Educational Centers, Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs), Corporate Firms, Fintech enterprises, and my current engagement with the tech startup, D'arlos Valley.

During this journey, I have undertaken substantial projects that involved the creation and consumption of RESTful services, designing complex database schemas, and proficiently working with databases such as MSSQL and MySQL. I have also honed my skills in technologies like Pomelo, Entity Framework, Dependency Injection, Encryption Methodologies, and the implementation of N-tier architecture.

In addition to this, I have actively employed concepts such as Unit of Work and Repository Architecture, demonstrating my commitment to developing robust and scalable software solutions. My journey continues as I explore and engage with various facets of the technology landscape.

Apart from my expertise in programming and web development, I delve deeply into research and excel in roles such as video editing, scriptwriting, and film direction. I have a passion for exploring new places, experimenting with cooking, embracing adventurous ideologies, and appreciating the beauty of music and nature. With a keen interest in sales, I possess the knack for selling practically anything I set my mind to. Additionally, my skills extend to learning, teaching, and coaching, showcasing strong leadership capabilities.
